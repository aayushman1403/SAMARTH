About Mumbai Safety Zone Predictor

The Mumbai Safety Zone Predictor is an innovative smart policing tool designed to visualize and predict safety levels across Mumbai's wards throughout the day.

Our Mission

To enhance public safety through data-driven insights, empowering law enforcement agencies to optimize resource allocation and implement preventive measures in high-risk areas.

How It Works

Our system uses advanced machine learning techniques to analyze historical crime data and predict safety levels for each ward in Mumbai based on time of day.

Data Sources
  • Mumbai OpenCity CSV datasets
  • Kaggle's "Crime in India" dataset
  • Police station reports
  • Demographic information
Technology Stack
  • ConvLSTM neural network models
  • Flask web framework
  • Leaflet.js for interactive maps
  • TensorFlow for prediction

Safety Level Indicators

Green Zone

Safe areas with low crime probability

Yellow Zone

Moderate risk, exercise caution

Red Zone

High risk, increased vigilance needed

Benefits for Law Enforcement

  • Optimize patrol routes based on predicted risk
  • Allocate resources efficiently across wards
  • Identify high-risk time periods for specific areas
  • Implement preventive measures in high-risk zones
  • Reduce response times through strategic positioning
  • Data-driven decision making for improved safety

Future Developments

We are continually improving our system with these upcoming features:

  • Real-time incident reporting integration
  • Mobile app for field officers with real-time alerts
  • Predictive analytics for specific crime types
  • Integration with emergency response systems
  • Community reporting feature for improved data collection
